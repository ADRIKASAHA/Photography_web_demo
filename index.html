Marsel van Oosten

Marsel van Oosten is a renowned nature photographer who has won numerous awards for his stunning wildlife and landscape photography. He is best known for his work capturing rare and endangered species in their natural habitats, and his images have been featured in prestigious publications around the world. In addition to his photography, he is also a passionate conservationist, using his art to raise awareness about the importance of preserving our planet's biodiversity.

Joel Sartore

Joel Sartore is an award-winning photographer and National Geographic Explorer who has dedicated his life to documenting endangered species and landscapes around the world. His work has been featured in magazines, books, and exhibits, and he has won numerous awards for his conservation photography.

Cristina Mittermeier

Cristina Mittermeier is a Mexican-American conservation photographer, National Geographic Explorer, and co-founder of the International League of Conservation Photographers and SeaLegacy. Her work has been featured in magazines, books, and exhibitions around the world, and she has won numerous awards for her photography and conservation work.

Florian Schultz

Florian Schultz is a well-known underwater photographer who has captured stunning images of marine life around the world. He is known for his ability to capture the beauty and diversity of the ocean, and his work has been featured in numerous publications and exhibitions. Schultz is also an advocate for ocean conservation and uses his photography to raise awareness of the need to protect our oceans and the creatures that inhabit them.
